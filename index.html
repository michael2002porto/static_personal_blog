The Holy Bible Religious The Holy Bible is the sacred text of Christianity. It is a collection of books written over a period of centuries by different authors. The Bible contains stories about the creation of the world, the history of the Jewish people, and the life and teachings of Jesus Christ.
A Man Called Ahok Biography A Man Called Ahok is a biography of Basuki Tjahaja Purnama, also known as Ahok, the former governor of Jakarta, Indonesia. The book tells the story of Ahok's rise from poverty to become one of the most powerful men in Indonesia. It also examines his controversial policies as governor and his impeachment trial.
Mantappu Jiwa by Jerome Polin Educational Mantappu Jiwa is a memoir by Jerome Polin, a popular Indonesian YouTuber and mathematics influencer. The book tells the story of Polin's journey from his humble beginnings in Indonesia to his success in Japan. Polin was born in 1998, just a few days after the Indonesian financial crisis. His family struggled financially, but they were determined to give him a good education. Polin excelled in mathematics and science, and he dreamed of one day studying in Japan. Polin worked hard to achieve his dream. He studied for hours every day, and he won several math competitions. He also applied for scholarships, and he was eventually awarded a full scholarship to Waseda University in Tokyo. Polin's story is one of perseverance and determination. It is a reminder that anything is possible if you set your mind to it.
